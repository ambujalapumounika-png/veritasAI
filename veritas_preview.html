METHODOLOGY

How VeritasAI Works

01
◈

Text Preprocessing

Raw input is normalized — lowercased, special characters stripped, whitespace collapsed into a clean token stream.

02
⬡

TF-IDF Vectorization

Dual vectorizers extract 15K word n-grams (1–3) and 8K character n-grams (3–5), capturing both semantic and stylistic patterns.

03
⬟

Ensemble Classification

Three models vote — Logistic Regression (45%), PAC (25%), and Linear SVC (30%) — with weighted probability averaging.

04
◇

Heuristic Signals

Rule-based NLP detects sensational language, excessive punctuation, all-caps abuse, and missing source attribution.

05
△

Confidence Scoring

ML ensemble probability (80%) blended with linguistic heuristic signal (20%) produces a final calibrated confidence score.

06
○

Explainable Output

Every prediction includes human-readable signal annotations showing exactly why the verdict was reached.
